About Me

I am a researcher in the Machine Learning Department of NEC Labs, America. Prior to this, I obtained my PhD from Northeastern University, and my master and bachelor degrees from Wuhan University. I interned in Adobe Research, NEC Labs, and JD AI Research, America. My research interests lie in machine learning and computer vision. In particular, I recently focus on learning from limited labeled data, including zero/few-shot learning, domain adaptation, unsupervised representation learning, etc.
