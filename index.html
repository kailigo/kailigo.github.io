About Me

I am a fourth-year PhD student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department, Northeastern University, advised by Prof. Yun Fu. My research interests lie in machine learning, computer vision and data mining. Specifically, I am now focusing on robust representation learning and the applications. Prior to this, I obtained my bachelor degree (2014) and master degree (2016) both in Wuhan University, where I worked on image matching and 3D reconstruction, under the supervision of Prof. Jian Yao.
